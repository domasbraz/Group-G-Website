Our Goal

Christmas is a time of family, cheer and giving, but not all can enjoy its festive cheer. There are many children out there suffering, why not lift their spirits by supporting them. There are charities out there that help children in need and our goal is to promote them.


Here are some charities you can donate to:
(Click the images for information.)

UNICEF A big organisation that works around world to help children in need. They offer many many different services for children to help them grow and learn.

Barnardos An Irish organisation set on helping children all over Ireland. They offer many different services from financial suport to personal development.

Jack and Jill An Irish organisation that aims at helping infants with mental development difficulties.

Make-A-Wish Ireland You may have heard of the popular American charity, "Make A Wish", well they have an affiliate here in Ireland. They try to bring happiness to children with life-threatening medical conditions by fulfilling their wish.

Children's Health Foundation Medical care can be expensive, this Irish organisation aims at providing vital funds to children so they get the proper health care they deserve.
